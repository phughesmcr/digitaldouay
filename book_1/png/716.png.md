twelue gouernours ouer al Israel, which serued victuals for the king and for his house: for euerie one ministred necessaries, eche man his moneth in the yeare. <sup>8</sup>And these are their names: Benhur in mount Ephraim. <sup>9</sup>Bendecar, in Macces, and in Salebim, and in Bethsames, and in Elon, and in Beth-hanan. <sup>10</sup>Benhesed in Aruboth: his was Socho, and al the land Epher.
